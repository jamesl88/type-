profile

A young and vibrant, London based studio providing services in architecture + design, undertaking projects of various scale and complexity ranging from residential and interior design, alteration and commercial fit-outs.

Prior to forming Type+, both partners have worked for various architectural practices across central London and the South-East with combined extensive experience in the residential and commercial sectors.

The notion of type+ is an artistic expression adding elements to the form. We believe that integrating the concept of addition creates new and interesting spaces when realising a complete building
