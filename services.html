services

We transform existing spaces; making them more functional whilst enhancing its character using a pragmatic and sensitive approach. We respect the original building features paying particular attention to linking of the new with the old; the architecture is altered but is not replaced. We collaborate with interior designers to ensure the combination of high quality design and bespoke finishes enhance the thinking behind the project.

We offer a service that works closely with the client to understand their vision at the outset; involving the communication of ideas through a collective design workshop which introduces a tailor-made service for each project. We draw emphasis on the client expectations, with each scheme treated in its own right. No project is too small or too big.
